# Welcome to the Official BlackRift Studios GitHub Organisation ;]

### Independent. Community Driven.

We are BlackRift Studios, an independent European modding and software development studio with staff and developers from around the world :DDD

We create projects because **we're passionate** about building them, not because someone told us to. Our goal has always been to deliver high quality experiences that put communities, developers, and players first, above money, trends, or outside influence.

**No investors.
No shareholders.
No corporate bureaucracy.**

Just a group of developers, builders, artists, and creators working together to build things we're proud of and share them with the public :]

---

##  What **WE** Value

In all honesty, we value our community more than anything.

You cannot be a studio without the people supporting you, which is why **YOU** will always come first.

We also:

* Believe transparency and open communication are required
* Remain fully independent with complete creative freedom
* Believe in quality over quantity
* Listen to community feedback whenever possible
* Focus on building projects that people genuinely enjoy, not that they hate

---

## Why Us???

Because we believe independent development ***still matters***.

Being independent allows us to focus on what truly benefits our projects and communities rather than chasing quarterly reports, investor expectations, or corporate trends that often turn great projects into soulless products like most modern triple A games xd...

Almost every major decision we discuss eventually comes down to one simple question:

> "Is this actually good for the community, and is it something people would genuinely want?"

If the answer is no, **we don't do it.**

Simple as that :]
